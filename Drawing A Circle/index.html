Li Sixian, Drawing A Circle, 2025

Through the act of drawing circles on paper for an extended period, the artist transforms this geometric shape into a metaphor for perfection.

During the three-hour performance, the audience is invited to ponder: Is perfection truly an attainable state, or merely an illusion existing only in the realm of ideals? And does our relentless pursuit of perfection hold any genuine value?
